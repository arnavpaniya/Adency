🎬 ADENCY – Complete Project Plan
📌 1. Introduction

ADENCY is a modern video production agency focused on creating:

UGC (User Generated Content)
Brand promotional videos
Social media content

The platform is designed to be simple, affordable, and trust-driven.

🔥 2. Unique Selling Proposition (USP)

“Pay Only After Delivery”

Unlike traditional agencies, ADENCY does not require upfront payment.
Clients only pay after receiving the final video.

🎯 Why this matters:
Builds instant trust
Reduces hesitation
Increases conversion rate
🎯 3. Objective of the Project
Build a high-conversion website
Showcase video work effectively
Provide clear pricing
Simplify the client onboarding process
Create a strong and modern brand identity
👥 4. Target Audience
Small businesses
Startups
Instagram brands
Content creators
Local service providers
🧠 5. Core Concept

The entire project is based on three key ideas:

1. Trust

2. Visual Impact
Portfolio videos

Creative collage visuals
3. Simplicity
Easy navigation
Minimal steps
Clear CTAs
